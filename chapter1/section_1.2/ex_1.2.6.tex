		$$
		2M - A
		= 2\begin{pmatrix}
		4 & 2 & 1 & 3 \\
		5 & 1 & 1 & 4 \\
		3 & 1 & 2 & 6
		\end{pmatrix} - \begin{pmatrix}
		5 & 3 & 1 & 2 \\
		6 & 2 & 1 & 5 \\
		1 & 0 & 3 & 3
		\end{pmatrix}
		= \begin{pmatrix}
		3 & 1 & 1 & 4 \\
		4 & 0 & 1 & 3 \\
		5 & 2 & 1 & 9
		\end{pmatrix}.
		$$
		Sum of all entries is $34$; hence $34$ suites were sold during the June Sale.
