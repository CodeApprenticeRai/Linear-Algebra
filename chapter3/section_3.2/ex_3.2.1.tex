Let $A := \begin{pmatrix}
0 & 0 \\
1 & 0
\end{pmatrix}$, then $\rank(A) = 1$. But we consider $A\times A = O$ where $\rank(O) = 0$. This means $\rank(O)\neq \rank(A)$.
\end{solution}
